About Mississauga Insider

Mississauga Insider is a local guide to Mississauga, Ontario: the top news, the best things to do, and the local businesses worth knowing. The site updates itself automatically every 6 hours.

How curation works

News headlines are gathered from public RSS feeds of local outlets. We show only a headline and a short excerpt, never the full article, with clear credit and a link to the original publisher, where you should read the full story. Event listings come from the Visit Mississauga events calendar (Tourism Mississauga). Weather data is from Open-Meteo. Business directory data and photos are from Google Maps.
