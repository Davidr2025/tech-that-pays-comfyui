ASD Band

3:00 p.m. · streetsville village square

Join us for a special live performance featuring ASD Band , an award-winning group of four talented performers on the autism spectrum: vocalist Rawan Tuffaha , keyboardist Ron Adea , vocalist and lead guitarist Jackson Begley , and drummer Spenser Murray . Formed in 2019, ASD Band made an impressive debut performing alongside rock legend Roger Hodgson and a 43-piece orchestra on Give A Little Bit . Since then, the band has continued to captivate audiences with their musicianship, creativity, and inspiring journey.

Free · Nature

Walk the boardwalk at Rattray Marsh Conservation Area

Rattray Marsh Conservation Area, Clarkson

A quiet lakeside nature reserve in Clarkson with boardwalk trails through marsh, woodland and beach habitat. One of the last natural shoreline marshes on Lake Ontario, great for birdwatching and an easy, flat walk any time of year.

Picnic and lakefront views at Jack Darling Memorial Park

Jack Darling Memorial Park, Lorne Park

A large waterfront park with open lawns, a splash pad, off-leash dog area, and direct access to the Waterfront Trail. Bring a picnic and watch the sailboats out of Lorne Park Yacht Club.

Port Credit waterfront

This stretch of the Lake Ontario Waterfront Trail runs past the Port Credit Lighthouse, marinas and Rotary Park, one of the most scenic free walks or bike rides in the city.
