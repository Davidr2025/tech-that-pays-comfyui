Walk the boardwalk at Rattray Marsh Conservation Area

Rattray Marsh Conservation Area, Clarkson

A quiet lakeside nature reserve in Clarkson with boardwalk trails through marsh, woodland and beach habitat. One of the last natural shoreline marshes on Lake Ontario, great for birdwatching and an easy, flat walk any time of year.
