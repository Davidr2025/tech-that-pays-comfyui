Mississauga’s air quality affected by wildfire smoke
Smoke from forest fires is expected to impact air quality in Mississauga, with conditions that may persist through the day. As smoke levels rise, so do health risks — making it important for residents to stay informed and take steps to protect themselves. Wildfire smoke can travel long distances and differs from typical urban air pollution. It contains fine particles and harmful gases that can enter the lungs and bloodstream, potentially causing inflammation and affecting overall health.
