Life Time has become one of the more recognizable names in premium fitness across North America, and its Mississauga club on Pepper Mill Court serves the west end of the city with one of the longer operating windows of any gym locally: doors open as early as 4 a.m. on weekdays and stay open until midnight, with a slightly later start and earlier close on weekends, 5 a.m. to 10 p.m.

The Life Time brand positions itself as more of an athletic club than a typical gym. Alongside standard strength and cardio equipment, locations are generally built around group fitness studios and a broader wellness experience aimed at members who want more than a bare-bones membership. The Mississauga club has built a substantial following locally, with well over a thousand reviews on Google.

If you're weighing your options for a fitness membership in Mississauga, hours this flexible are worth factoring in, especially for early risers heading in before work or anyone squeezing in a late-evening session. The club is located at 3055 Pepper Mill Ct, just off the west end of the city, and can be reached directly at (905) 363-0200 for current membership details, tours, or class schedules.
